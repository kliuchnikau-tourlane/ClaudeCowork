### Meeting Purpose & Context

- Moving from asynchronous to synchronous status updates for high-level initiatives
- Information loss and misalignment in current async process
  - People interpret messages differently
  - No opportunity for clarifying questions
  - Confusion about status meaning, risks, and remaining work
- Focus on initiative-level updates, not ticket-level details

### Process Changes

- Synchronous discussion of urgent/pressing initiatives
- Meeting outcomes inform written status updates posted 1-2 days later
- Prioritize initiatives that are off-track or need attention
- Green-light initiatives can remain as status updates
- Accountability clarification needed
  - Some initiatives lack clear ownership
  - Gap between engineering and product delivery responsibility

### Current Initiative Status Questions

- Content automation: unclear progress and remaining work
- Outsourcing: getting green light but missing details on completion
- Expedia certification: external dependency creating risk
  - Progress made in last 2 days with many closures
  - Still see risk due to Expedia dependency
- Smart booking: discovery process status unclear

### Meeting Structure & Frequency

- Bi-weekly cadence initially
- May reduce to monthly once process established
- Consider splitting by domains (Speedy/Flamingo vs other teams)
- Focus on three key questions per initiative:
  1. Are we on track for cycle one delivery?
  2. Is progress as expected or delayed?
  3. What risks/blockers exist?
